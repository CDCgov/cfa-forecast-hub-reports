The CovidHub ensemble predicts that for the week ending June 27, 2026, the weekly percentage of new emergency department (ED) visits due to COVID-19 in the United States will be 0.084% (95% prediction interval 0.04-0.18%). The median forecast suggests a decrease from 0.09%, which was reported for the week ending June 13, 2026 (the most recent week of reporting).

Ensemble forecasting also predicts that the number of new laboratory-confirmed COVID-19 hospital admissions in the United States during the week ending June 27, 2026 will be approximately 830 (420-2200). The median forecast suggests a decrease compared to the 850 admissions so far reported for the week ending June 13, 2026.

Overview: Forecasted percent of ED visits and new hospital admissions due to COVID-19. This week, 3 modeling groups contributed 3 forecasts of ED visits and 8 modeling groups contributed 9 forecasts of hospital admissions that were eligible for inclusion in the ensemble forecasts for at least one jurisdiction.

What does the figure show?: The figure shows the percentage of COVID-19 emergency department visits each week reported from October 01, 2022 through June 13, 2026, number of new laboratory-confirmed COVID-19 hospital admissions reported in the United States each week from August 08, 2020 through June 13, 2026, and forecasted new COVID-19 emergency department visits and hospital admissions per week for this week and the next 2 weeks through July 04, 2026.
